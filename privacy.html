1. Introduction

Rotinely ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our iOS application ("App").

By using Rotinely, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Rotinely, you may create:

  • Routine names, descriptions, and configurations
  • Step titles, durations, and order
  • Routine execution history and completion records
  • Statistics and progress data

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • App version and usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Crash reports and performance data (if you opt in via iOS settings)
  • Purchase and subscription status (anonymized transaction identifiers)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or contact information (unless you contact us)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Health or biometric data

4. Data Retention

Your routine data is retained on your device for as long as you use the App:

  • Active Use: Data persists until you manually delete it or uninstall the App
  • Uninstallation: All local data is permanently deleted when you uninstall Rotinely
  • iCloud Data: Remains in your iCloud account until you delete it or disable sync
  • Crash Reports: Retained by Apple for up to 25 months per their policies

5. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • Display your routines in widgets and Live Activities
  • Calculate statistics and track your progress
  • Process subscription purchases (via Apple)
  • Improve App stability and fix bugs (via anonymized crash reports)

When you start a routine, Rotinely uses iOS Live Activities and widgets to display your progress:

8. Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Local Encryption: Data stored using iOS secure storage (SwiftData with device encryption)
  • iCloud Encryption: All synced data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • No Remote Access: We cannot access your data as it never reaches our servers
  • Secure Coding: We follow Apple's security best practices and guidelines

9.2 European Economic Area (EEA) Residents - GDPR Rights

If you are in the EEA, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Legal Basis: We process data based on your consent (by using the App) and legitimate interest (providing the service)
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing activities
  • Right to Complain: Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise most rights directly. For assistance, contact us at the email below.

9.3 California Residents - CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights:

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect, use, and disclose
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights
  • Do Not Sell: We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties

To exercise your rights, contact us at the email address below.

10. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

If you purchase a subscription:

  • All payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store
  • We receive anonymized transaction data (purchase date, product ID, subscription status)
  • We do NOT receive your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details
  • Apple's Privacy Policy and Terms govern payment processing
  • Your routine data remains on your device regardless of subscription status

11. Children's Privacy

Rotinely is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

12. International Data Transfers

Since your data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account, there are no international data transfers by us. Apple may transfer iCloud data internationally according to their privacy policy and applicable data protection agreements.
