Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Rotinely ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS app.

Data We Collect

Routine Data

Information you create within the app:

  • Routine names and descriptions
  • Step titles and durations
  • Routine execution history
  • Statistics and progress data

Technical Data

  • Device type and iOS version
  • App usage statistics (anonymous)
  • Crash reports (if enabled)

How We Store Your Data

  • Local Storage: All routine data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData
  • iCloud Sync: If enabled, data syncs via Apple's iCloud (encrypted by Apple)
  • App Groups: Widget data shared locally between app and widget extension
  • No Server: We do not store your data on our servers

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use tracking or analytics that identify you personally
  • Access your data - it stays on your device

Apple may process data for:

  • iCloud sync (if you enable it)
  • In-app purchase processing
  • App Store functionality

Live Activities

When you start a routine, we use Apple's Live Activities feature to display your progress. This data:

  • Stays on your device
  • Is managed by iOS
  • Disappears when the routine ends

Subscriptions

If you purchase a subscription:

  • Payment is processed by Apple
  • We receive anonymous transaction data
  • Apple's privacy policy applies
  • Your routine data remains on your device regardless of subscription status

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to delete all local data
  • Export: [Add if you implement this feature]
  • Control Sync: Disable iCloud sync in iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

Rotinely is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes via app update notes.
